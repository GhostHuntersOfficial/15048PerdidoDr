The Haunting on 15048 Perdido Dr

The Haunting of the Perdido House - Investigated by Tyler Jacobs - Date: October 15th, 2015

Hello, my name is Tyler Jacobs. I’m not a professional, but I’ve been obsessed with ghosts and the supernatural since I was a kid. I always wanted to prove that ghosts are real. That’s why, in August 2016, I decided to visit a house in my local aera. I found an article in the newspaper talking about hearing strange voices in the house at night. Seeing this as an opportunity to prove ghosts were real I decided to investigate. When I called the phone number at the bottom of the page the man who picked up was the owner. He told me his family was currently living in a hotel because they did not feel comfortable living there anymore. I asked if I could investigate to see what was really going on and he obliged, but warned me to be careful.

The pervious owner told me his family had been living there for a little less than a year when "Stuff got weird"

The family had reported the following...

  • The Sound of chains
  • Scratching on doors
  • Temporary Posessions
  • Flickering Lights
  • A figure standing in the Dark
  • Unlocking of doors

My Time In The House

I arrived at the Perdido residence late in the evening. The house had 2 floors with the bedrooms upstairs and the kitchen, living room and pantry downstairs. I went inside alone, just me and my flashlight. The air inside felt… heavy. Like something was watching me. It was colder than outside, even though it wasn’t supposed to be.

I started with a basic walkthrough, I set up a few motion sensors and turned on my EMF reader. I was trying to capture any unusual sounds or movement, but nothing happened at first. Then, something strange happened. I heard footsteps. But it wasnt mine... it was someone else's. They were soft, slow and rythmic. I thought it might be an animal, but it didn’t sound human. This was something heavier. Something that didn’t belong.

Against my better judgement I set my things up to stay overnight in the master bedroom.

Around 10 that night I was geting ready for sleep when it happened. All of a sudden my EMF reader when to 5 (The highest It can go) and a loud slamming came from downstairs. I had to investigate. the moment I steped into the pantry room I saw it laying there. There was a dead body. I ran away so fast I didnt even collect my things I just left.

There is a demon in the house DO NOT LIVE HERE OR YOU COULD DIE
